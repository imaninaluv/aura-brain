# Roadmap

Version: 1.0
Status: Locked
Last Updated: 2026-07-05

---

# Vision

Aura AI exists to become the world's most intelligent AI Social Media Growth Operating System.

Rather than functioning as another AI writing assistant, Aura AI aims to become a complete growth companion that helps creators consistently publish, engage, analyse, and grow their businesses.

Every future decision should support this vision.

---

# Mission

Help creators spend less time managing social media and more time growing their business.

Aura AI should remove repetitive work while preserving the creator's unique voice.

Creators should always remain the decision maker.

Aura AI simply makes better decisions easier.

---

# Product Evolution

Aura AI will evolve gradually.

Each version expands naturally without changing the core experience.

Users should never feel forced to relearn the product.

# Future Evolution

Aura AI will continue improving its intelligence rather than simply adding more features.

Future improvements may include:

- Better content generation
- Better reader psychology
- Smarter Scout recommendations
- Smarter Planner suggestions
- Better performance analysis
- Stronger writing personalisation

Every improvement should strengthen HERO and AURA rather than replacing them.

---

# Scout Evolution

Future Scout capabilities may include:

- Discover trending topics
- Discover influencers
- Discover viral discussions
- Discover frequently asked questions within a niche

Scout should eventually become an intelligent research assistant rather than only an engagement finder.

---

# Planner Evolution

Planner will gradually evolve into an AI Content Planner.

Future examples:

"Wednesday has no scheduled content."

"Generate one Selling thread?"

Planner should eventually recommend content opportunities automatically.

---

# Stats Evolution

Statistics should move beyond reporting.

Future versions should provide intelligent recommendations.

Examples:

- Best posting time
- Best performing topics
- Best performing Writing Mode
- Performance trends
- Content recommendations

The objective is helping creators make better decisions—not simply displaying more numbers.

---

# Adaptive Intelligence

Aura AI should become more personal over time.

Future versions may automatically learn:

- Writing preferences
- Editing behaviour
- Favourite tone
- Preferred CTA style
- Preferred content structure

The longer creators use Aura AI, the more natural the generated content should feel.

# Business Model

Aura AI follows a one-time lifetime purchase model.

---

## Free Trial

New users may begin with a 7-day Pro Trial.

A valid payment method is required before starting the trial.

No payment is charged during the trial period.

Reminder emails will be sent before the trial ends, allowing users to cancel before any payment is collected.

The objective is giving creators enough time to experience Aura AI before making a purchasing decision.

# Success Metrics

Aura AI measures success differently.

Primary success is not downloads.

Primary success is creator growth.

Key indicators include:

- Weekly active creators
- Weekly publishing consistency
- Weekly engagement activity
- Creator retention
- Business growth enabled by Aura AI

If creators consistently grow using Aura AI, the product is succeeding.

# Product Principles

Aura AI will never sacrifice quality for speed.

Aura AI will never replace the creator.

Aura AI exists to amplify creators—not imitate them.

Every future feature should continue supporting these principles.

# Things Aura AI Will Never Do

Aura AI will never:

- Spam content
- Generate fake engagement
- Purchase followers
- Sacrifice quality for speed
- Over-automate until creators lose their unique identity

Trust is more valuable than automation.

Long-term creator growth is more valuable than short-term numbers.
