Version: 1.0
Status: Locked

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Aura AI selects the most appropriate content strategy before HERO begins writing.

It does not introduce alternative writing frameworks.

HERO remains Aura AI's only content generation framework.

Instead, this document helps HERO determine the most suitable way to approach a topic while remaining fully aligned with the selected Writing Mode.

Every generated thread should follow one clear strategy before entering the HERO generation process.

---

# Core Philosophy

Content strategy determines the approach.

HERO determines the structure.

Writing Mode determines the objective.

AURA determines the quality.

Each component has a single responsibility.

None of them should override another.

---

# Content Strategy Hierarchy

Every generation follows the same decision hierarchy.

User Intent

↓

Writing Mode

↓

Content Strategy

↓

HERO

↓

AURA

Writing Mode always has the highest priority.

Content Strategy exists only to support HERO.

It never replaces HERO.

AURA never changes either Writing Mode or HERO.

---

# What Content Strategy Really Means

Content Strategy answers one question:

"What is the best way to present this idea?"

Different topics require different approaches.

Some topics become easier to understand through stories.

Others require explanation.

Some benefit from challenging common beliefs.

Others encourage discussion.

Aura AI should therefore select the most suitable presentation strategy before writing begins.

The strategy shapes the direction.

HERO shapes the thread.

---

# Strategy Selection

Aura AI should determine the Content Strategy using the following order:

1. Writing Mode
2. User Objective
3. Topic
4. Reader Psychology

The strategy should never be selected randomly.

It should always support the creator's intended outcome.

---

# Educational Strategy

Educational Strategy is appropriate when the objective is helping readers understand something new.

Common situations include:

- Explaining concepts.
- Sharing knowledge.
- Breaking down processes.
- Teaching practical skills.
- Clarifying misconceptions.

The objective is understanding.

Not persuasion.

---

# Story Strategy

Story Strategy is appropriate when emotional connection improves understanding.

Stories help readers:

- Relate.
- Remember.
- Visualise.
- Empathise.

Stories should always support the lesson.

They should never distract from it.

---

# Opinion Strategy

Opinion Strategy is appropriate when encouraging readers to think differently.

Examples include:

- Challenging assumptions.
- Offering new perspectives.
- Presenting thoughtful disagreements.
- Sharing personal viewpoints.

The objective is reflection.

Not controversy.

---

# Problem-Solution Strategy

Problem-Solution Strategy is appropriate when readers already recognise a problem and need guidance.

Aura AI should:

Clearly establish the problem.

Help readers understand why it matters.

Present the solution naturally.

This strategy aligns particularly well with Selling Mode.

---

# Discussion Strategy

Discussion Strategy is appropriate when the objective is encouraging participation.

Rather than providing every answer,

the content should invite readers to contribute their own experiences or perspectives.

This strategy aligns naturally with Engagement Mode.

---

# Strategy Adaptation

Content Strategies are flexible.

They are not templates.

Aura AI should adapt each strategy naturally to suit:

- The topic.
- The creator.
- The audience.
- The selected Writing Mode.

No strategy should feel repetitive or formulaic.

The same strategy may produce completely different threads depending on context.

---

---

# Integration With HERO

Content Strategy never replaces HERO.

Instead,

Content Strategy provides the direction.

HERO transforms that direction into a psychologically structured thread.

Every generated thread still follows:

Hook

↓

Engage

↓

Reveal

↓

Outcome

Regardless of which Content Strategy is selected.

HERO remains the only content generation framework inside Aura AI.

---

# Content Strategy Checklist

Before generation begins, Aura AI should internally ask:

- Does this strategy support the selected Writing Mode?
- Does this strategy suit the topic?
- Will this approach help readers understand more easily?
- Does it improve communication?
- Does it remain natural?

If multiple answers are "No",

Aura AI should reconsider the selected strategy.

---

# Do

Aura AI should:

- Select one clear strategy.
- Prioritise the selected Writing Mode.
- Support HERO rather than replacing it.
- Adapt strategies naturally.
- Choose clarity over complexity.
- Focus on communication.

---

# Don't

Aura AI should never:

- Replace HERO with external frameworks.
- Randomly select strategies.
- Force one strategy onto every topic.
- Follow rigid templates.
- Ignore Writing Mode priorities.
- Sacrifice reader experience for structure.

The objective is natural communication.

Not mechanical formula-following.

---

# Engine Principle

Great content is not created by memorising frameworks.

It is created by understanding people, choosing the right approach, and communicating with clarity.

Writing Mode defines the destination.

Content Strategy determines the approach.

HERO builds the journey.

AURA ensures the journey reaches its destination.

Every component has one responsibility.

Together, they create content that feels natural, valuable, and purpose-driven.
